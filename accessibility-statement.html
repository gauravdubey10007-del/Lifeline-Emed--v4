Accessibility Statement

Last updated: 2026

Lifeline Emed Companies LLC is committed to making knowledge freely and equitably accessible. We strive to ensure that our website and publications are usable by the widest possible audience, regardless of ability or technology.

Our Commitment

  • We aim to align with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA where reasonably practicable.
  • We use clear structure, readable typography, and sufficient color contrast.
  • Our open-access model removes subscription barriers so content is freely available worldwide.

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We continue to review and improve our website to enhance usability for people who rely on assistive technologies such as screen readers and keyboard navigation.

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier or need content in an alternative format, please contact us at info@lifelineemed.com. We welcome your feedback and will do our best to provide the information you need.
