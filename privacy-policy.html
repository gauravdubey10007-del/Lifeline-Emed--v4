Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Lifeline Emed Companies LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us through this website and our publishing services for the American Journal of Research and Innovation (AJRI).

Information We Collect

  • Contact details you provide — such as name, email address, affiliation, and message content — when you submit a form, create an account, or correspond with us.
  • Manuscript and submission information provided in connection with peer review and publication.
  • Basic technical data (such as browser type and pages visited) collected automatically to operate and improve the site.

How We Use Information

  • To process and respond to enquiries, submissions, and collaboration proposals.
  • To manage author accounts and the peer-review and publication process.
  • To communicate editorial decisions, updates, and relevant scholarly information.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of our services.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Information may be shared with editorial board members and reviewers as necessary for peer review, and with service providers who support our operations under appropriate confidentiality obligations.

Data Retention & Security

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above and apply reasonable safeguards to protect it. No method of transmission over the internet is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at info@lifelineemed.com.

Contact
