About Lifeline Emed

A United States–based organization dedicated to advancing scholarly publishing, healthcare innovation, scientific research, education, and international collaboration.

Our Vision

To become a globally recognized leader in scholarly publishing, scientific advancement, healthcare innovation, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Our Mission

To provide ethical, accessible, and high-quality platforms for researchers, healthcare professionals, educators, engineers, innovators, and institutions to publish, collaborate, and disseminate impactful knowledge.
