This Cookie Policy explains how Lifeline Emed Companies LLC uses cookies and similar technologies on this website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites function and remember your preferences.

How We Use Cookies

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to operate, such as maintaining your session.
  • Preference cookies — remember choices you make to improve your experience.
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.

Managing Cookies

Most browsers let you control or delete cookies through their settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect how the website functions.
